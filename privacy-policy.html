Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Our Commitment: myHealthCopilot is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your health data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud services.

1. Overview

myHealthCopilot ("the App") is a personal health management application that helps you track healthcare appointments, medications, and health conditions. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Storage: On-Device Only

All your health data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal health information.

  • Local Database: Your data is stored in an encrypted SQLCipher database on your device
  • Audio Recordings: Visit recordings are processed and stored locally
  • Transcriptions: Generated using on-device AI (Whisper) and encrypted at rest
  • Insurance Cards: Images are stored in encrypted local storage

3. Information We Collect

The App allows you to store the following types of information locally on your device:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Appointment details Track healthcare visits Encrypted on device
Audio recordings Record and transcribe visits Encrypted on device
Medications Manage prescriptions and dosages Encrypted on device
Health conditions Track ongoing health topics Encrypted on device
Insurance card photos Quick reference to coverage Encrypted on device
Location (optional) Find nearby healthcare providers Not stored; used only in real-time

4. On-Device AI Processing

The App uses on-device artificial intelligence for several features. This processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Speech-to-Text: Audio recordings are transcribed using on-device Whisper AI with Core ML acceleration
  • Visit Summaries: Transcriptions are summarized using Apple Intelligence (iOS) or Google Gemini Nano (Android)
  • OCR Scanning: Text recognition for pill bottles and appointment slips uses on-device processing

No audio, transcriptions, or health summaries are sent to external servers.

5. Optional Cloud Features

The App may optionally use cloud AI services for enhanced medication information (such as descriptions of common medications). If enabled:

  • Only generic medication names are sent (e.g., "sertraline"), not your personal health data
  • No personally identifiable information is transmitted
  • You can disable this feature to use fully offline operation

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following platform services:

  • Apple HealthKit (iOS): If you grant permission, the App can read health data from HealthKit. This data is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • Maps: When you tap on an address, it opens in your preferred maps application. The address is sent to the maps provider.

7. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using SQLCipher (AES-256)
  • Biometric Protection: Optional Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode required to access the App
  • Secure Storage: Encryption keys are stored in the device's secure enclave
  • No Transmission: Health data never leaves your device

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You have full control:

  • Delete individual records at any time within the App
  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App
  • Export your data before deletion if desired

Since we do not store your data on any servers, there is nothing to delete on our end.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • Access: View all your data within the App
  • Export: Export your data in standard formats
  • Delete: Remove any or all data at any time
  • Portability: Your data is yours; it never leaves your device without your action

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the effective date above.
